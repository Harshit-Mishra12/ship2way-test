Add Inventory Warehouse

An Inventory Warehouse is a physical location that houses your inventory. You likely have the Warehouse Locations broken down into three dimensions (e.g. aisle, shelf, and bin), but you’re able to define between one and four dimensions, depending on the complexity of your Warehouse Location numbering system.
